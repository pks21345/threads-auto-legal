Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: 2026-04-06

To request deletion of Threads Auto data associated with your authorized account connection, send an email to 54321pks@naver.com with the subject line Threads Auto Data Deletion Request.

What to Include

  • Your Threads or Meta account identifier used during authorization
  • The email address you want us to contact
  • Any additional context needed to identify the connected record

What Happens Next

We will review the request, confirm the account mapping, and delete applicable operational records that are not required for security or legal retention.
