Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-06

Threads Auto is an internal MVP integration used to draft and publish approved Threads posts for the operator's own company account.

Information We Process

For MVP operation, Threads Auto may process account identifiers, access tokens, post payloads, publish results, timestamps, and operational audit records needed to publish and troubleshoot approved content.

How We Use Information

We use this information only to authenticate the Threads integration, publish approved content, maintain an audit trail, and diagnose operational failures.

Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be processed by infrastructure providers required to operate the service and by Meta when the Threads API is used.

Retention

Operational data is retained only as long as needed for debugging, audit, and service continuity, then removed or rotated according to internal operational needs.
